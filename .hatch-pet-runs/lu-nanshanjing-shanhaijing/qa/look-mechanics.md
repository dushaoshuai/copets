# Lu look mechanics

Keep the four hooves and lower torso registered. Eyes lead, followed by the deer-like muzzle and horned head; ears, mane fins, back plates, wing-like shoulder fins, and long curled tail follow subtly according to their attachments. Preserve the cream, teal, gold, and pastel scale pattern.

- 000 up: eyes and muzzle pitch up; ears/horns lift; neck lengthens slightly.
- 090 screen-right: muzzle and pupils point right; left head/body side is more visible; tail tip lags left.
- 180 down: eyes and muzzle lower; neck compresses slightly; ears angle down.
- 270 screen-left: muzzle and pupils point left; right head/body side is more visible; tail tip lags right.

Keep steps even and grounded. Do not rotate the whole creature, detach fins, or change horn/tail geometry abruptly.
