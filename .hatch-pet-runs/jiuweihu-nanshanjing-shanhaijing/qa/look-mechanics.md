# Jiuweihu look mechanics

Anchor all four paws and the central torso. The large eyes lead, then the fox muzzle, head, ears, and upper chest turn; nine tails remain a coherent fan and follow with subtle delayed sway without changing count or attachment. Preserve the orange-white markings and flame-like tail shapes.

- 000 up: pupils and muzzle pitch up; ears lift; tail fan remains centered.
- 090 screen-right: muzzle/nose and pupils turn right; left cheek and body side become more visible; tails lag slightly left.
- 180 down: gaze and muzzle lower; ears relax; head tucks toward chest.
- 270 screen-left: muzzle/nose and pupils turn left; right cheek and body side become more visible; tails lag slightly right.

Use a continuous clockwise arc; no tail-count drift, full-body turntable rotation, scale pop, or detached effects.
