# Chiru look mechanics

Anchor the curled fish tail and lower body at the same baseline. The large eyes lead, then the face and fin-like crest turn or pitch toward the target; the torso bends only slightly and the tail follows with a soft delayed curve. Preserve the red-orange scales, cream face, flame fins, and compact upright identity.

- 000 up: pupils and face pitch upward; crest lifts; underside remains centered.
- 090 screen-right: nose, pupils, and face turn clearly right; left side of the head/body becomes more visible; tail lags left.
- 180 down: pupils and face pitch downward; crest compresses slightly; body remains anchored.
- 270 screen-left: nose, pupils, and face turn clearly left; right side becomes more visible; tail lags right.

Use even 22.5-degree changes. No whole-sprite rotation, replacement eyes, scale changes, or tail teleporting.
