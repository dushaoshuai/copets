# Xuangui look mechanics

Anchor the turtle feet, shell, and lower body. The yellow eyes and beaked serpent head lead; the long neck bends and yaws naturally while the red crest follows. The black-red shell stays stable, and the snake-like tail follows with a delayed continuous curve while remaining attached.

- 000 up: eyes and beak aim upward; neck extends; crest lifts.
- 090 screen-right: beak tip and pupils clearly cross right; left neck/head side becomes more visible; tail lags left.
- 180 down: eyes and beak aim down; neck compresses toward the shell.
- 270 screen-left: beak tip and pupils clearly cross left; right neck/head side becomes more visible; tail lags right.

Use even neck curvature changes. No shell rotation, whole-sprite tilt, replacement eyes, or tail detachment.
