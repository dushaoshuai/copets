# Qitu look mechanics

Anchor the shared chicken body and feet. All three heads coordinate toward the same target while retaining three distinct neck attachments: eyes and beaks lead, necks bend in parallel with slight natural offsets, combs follow, and folded wings remain stable. Never merge, duplicate, or reorder the heads.

- 000 up: all three beaks and pupils angle upward; necks extend slightly.
- 090 screen-right: all beaks/pupils clearly aim screen-right; left sides of heads/necks become more visible.
- 180 down: all three gazes and beaks lower; necks tuck modestly.
- 270 screen-left: all beaks/pupils clearly aim screen-left; right sides of heads/necks become more visible.

Each 22.5-degree step moves all three heads coherently. No whole-body spin, head-count drift, crossing necks, or independent contradictory gazes.
